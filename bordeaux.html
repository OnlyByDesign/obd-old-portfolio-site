The Bordeaux Wine Festival

is a concept festival for wine connoisseurs old and young taking place on the outskirts of Bordeaux, France. I was tasked with designing a mailer piece that would adequitely explain the event, as well as being attractive enough for people to want to attend. The project included an informational trifold, invitational ticket, and an infographic poster showcasing the basics of wine tasting.

Art Director

Soonduk Krebs

Kuenstler was the perfect typeface for this project, when laid out with a careful attention to detail.

Since the project was meant for all ages, the type had to be easy to read for every degree of eye-sight. High contrast colors and bolds iconography helped achieve this effect, which helped in keeping the text legible and dramatic.

While the more experienced connoisseurs may know the basics, the new ones may not. This festival is meant for people of all levels of skill, so who wouldn't like a little break-down?

An informational poster was included with the project, as a jumping off point for newbies. With bold iconography in mind, the focus of the poster was a quick and easy understanding of wine tasting and the factors that contribute.
