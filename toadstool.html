A concept catalog series focused on mushroom hunting

and the most common varieties of mushrooms one can encounter. Geared towards mushroom hunters new and traveled, the series is a fun look into the world of fungi. A 3-part catalogue series, which Included a carefully-crafted interface, bold photography and typography, and even an infographic series.

Art Director

Soonduk Krebs

With so many different kinds of mushrooms, it's easy to get distracted by the images alone.

Using Univers and Bodoni, a careful balance was achieved between the two typefaces and the found photos that were used throughout the catalogues. A careful layout and bold type treatment were used to draw the readers' attention towards important information, while the bold colors in imagery were used to grab it.

A fun challenge of the project was showcasing the mushrooms and ther descriptors in a way that was easily digestible, birthing the block information system utilized.
