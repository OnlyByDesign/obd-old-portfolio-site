Hand Lettering is a comprehensive concept catalog

spanning a multitude typographic mediums, featuring several different artists from the genre. Challenged with creating a project where an artist is the concept, I started with laying out the catalog from just the copy and imagery provided. Hand drawn type, careful attention to detail, and excessive fretting over type systems led to the creation of this piece.

Art Director

Kelly Holohan

I created my own hand-done typographic elements to be featured in the catalog.

A grid pattern was used throughout the catalog as a nod to how typographers, illustrators, and hand-letterers work on their pieces - in almost all articles, one unifying factor is the extensive use of grids on everthing from the tiddle to the whole piece.
