Business Wire is an internationally recognized

news sourcing company, with branches all over the world. Business Wire came to Marcolina Design continuously, and I was tasked with creating icon sets, and more importantly, the landing page for the launch of a new product - Interactive Media.

Art Director

Dan Marcolina

Fully responsive, the website features on scroll videos and examples of the Interactive Media, which is produced through WireWax.

I used HubSpot, custom animations, and JavaScript to create an immersive experience.

On-scroll enabled videos, examples of Interactive Media built through the WireWax platform, and a quick breakdown of user statistics along with key features gives the user a quick look into what's to come from the product.

Iconography produced for Business Wire ranged from simple icon sets for web pages and print media, to infographics and the like.
