This issue of the concept magazine 'Process'

includes a feature article about the designer James Victore and his work. The challenge for this project was to build a magazine article with the concept being an individual artist and the impact their work has. By creating many assets by hand, with inspiration taken directly from the work of Victore, the results were bold and in-your-face; exactly the kind of treatment needed for a project like this.

Art Director

Abby Bennette

With emotionally moving imagery, James Victore really does shove your face into social and political issues that are pressing, and has no regrets. I tried to achieve the same effect.

Using splash patterns, juxtaposed type placement, and bold illustrations and type treatments, I achieved a careful balance between bold and beautiful.
