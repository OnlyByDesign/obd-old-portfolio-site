Honey Apparel is a concept clothing line designed to give men a new, cheap alternative to high end fashion without sacrificing style. Challenged with the task to design in a very specific neighe of the fashion world, I was able to build the brand by focusing on a minimalist, bold but not weighted. The branding package included extended into packaging, with an expansion in mind for the future.

Art Director

Paul Sheriff

Careful type treatment and heavy consideration for the logo and how it works with white space.

Using a simple dover image, the mark was created after heavy customization and tireless efforts for a well thought-out, interesting, and minimal approach to men's fashion. The branding was meant to feel high end, but old-fashioned.
